UX/UI Bookmarklet - Note Collector

A tool for quickly collecting UX/UI observations while browsing websites

How to Use:

  1. Drag the link below to your browser's bookmarks bar
  2. While viewing any site, highlight text with a UX/UI issue
  3. Click the bookmark in your bookmarks bar
  4. In the pop-up window, add a note and save
  5. Return here to view all collected notes
